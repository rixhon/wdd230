Visual Hierarchy

TESLA


www.tesla.com

Tesla's mobile site has a good visual hierarchy. The home page features a prominent image and below a call-to-action for direct sales. The message is clear, with big headings and harmonious subheadings. The visual hierarchy is clear. This lets users quickly know where to find the information they're looking for and take action.

Rule of Thirds

NATIONAL GEOGRAPHIC


www.nationalgeographic.com

Rule of Thirds creates an attractive and intuitive visual hierarchy. With easy navigation and clear presentation of content. With the featured image and article title being positioned at intersections or dividing lines. This draws the user's attention to the highlighted content and helps them quickly find the information they are looking for.

Hick's Law

AMAZON


www.amazon.com

Like any other e-commerce, the screen has a large volume of content, this can cause problems when it comes to navigation and decision making, users can feel overwhelmed and indecisive, which can result in a negative browsing experience.
